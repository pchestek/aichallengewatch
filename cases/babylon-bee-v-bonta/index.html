Summary

The Babylon Bee, a satirical news website, and Kelly Chang Rickert, a California attorney and blogger, challenged California’s AB 2839 and AB 2655 as unconstitutional restrictions on political satire and parody. The laws would prohibit or require labeling of AI-generated content depicting candidates, even when clearly satirical.

The case was consolidated with Kohls v. Bonta in October 2024. The district court granted summary judgment for plaintiffs on August 29, 2025, permanently enjoining AB 2839. California appealed to the Ninth Circuit.
